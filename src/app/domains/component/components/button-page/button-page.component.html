A button is an interactive element that initiates specific actions when clicked, tapped, or activated, guiding users toward intended outcomes within an application or website. Buttons are often labeled to indicate their function—such as "Submit," "Cancel," or "Learn More"—giving users clear feedback about the action that will occur. Primary Use a primary button to call attention to a form submission or to highlight the most important call to action on a page. Primary buttons should only appear once per area, but not every screen needs a primary button.
